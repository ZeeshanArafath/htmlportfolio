My Top 3 Best Books

Kitab Tawheed

Goes in depth in the explanation of Tawheed al-Uloohiyyah

Aqeedah Wasitiyyah

Goes in depth in the explanation of Tawheed al-Asmaa w'as Siffaat - and other aspects of Aqeedah from the Aqeedah of Ahlus Sunnah w'al Jamaa'ah

Aqeedah Tahawiyyah

from at-Tahawi, explanation by Ibn Abi al-Izz al-Hanafi, goes through the Aqeedah of Ahlus Sunnah - must be studied under a scholar
